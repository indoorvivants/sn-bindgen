## C++ definitions are not supported

At this point it's unclear whether or when they will be supported, as C++ is infinitely more complex and not designed for interop. 

We welcome concrete proposals (in [Discussions](https://github.com/indoorvivants/sn-bindgen/discussions) or [Issues](https://github.com/indoorvivants/sn-bindgen/issues)) on how to start chipping away at this.
